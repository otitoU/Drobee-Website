Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the preview?

On device previews give you a good sense of fit and color. HD renders are highly realistic using advanced AI.

What phones are supported?

iOS 15+ and Android 8+. Most phones from the last 4 years work great.

Do I need to upload face photos?

One body photo is all you need. We never require face photos and you can delete your photo anytime.

What happens to my images?

Photos stay on your phone by default. Only HD renders are processed on our servers, then automatically deleted after 30 days.

How do credits work?

Each HD render uses one credit. Pro includes 50 monthly credits. You can buy more anytime for $0.50 each.
